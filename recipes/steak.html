Steak

This is a description of a Steak
Steak is a variety of beef dish with meat, sauce and some appetizer. Steak is worldwide dish by origin and it is very mouth-watery

Ingredients

    Potatoes
    Beef
    Sauce
    Cheese

Steps

    Grill your meat
    Cook or fry your potatoes
    Cut your beef into pieces
    Prepare the sauce
    Serve the potatoes with beef and sauce
