Lasagna

This is a description of a lasagna
Lasagna is a variety of pasta dish with meat or vegetables and sauce. Lasagna is Greek dish by origin and it is very tasteful

Ingredients

    Pasta
    Beef
    Sauce
    Cheese

Steps

    Prepare your meat sauce
    Cook (or don't cook) your noodles
    Make your noodles into thin sheets
    Prepare your cheese and finish the sauce
    Assemble and oven heat for sometime
